Năm mới chào em. - 01/01/25 (English Version)

"My beloved,

Happy New Year, my dear.

Look at the fireworks - aren't they mesmerizing?

Each spark that ignites and fades, mirrors the moments of the past year - brief yet infinitely beautiful. My heart overflows with joy and profound gratitude. I'm thankful for how gently the past year has treated us, for giving me the most precious gift of all:

Your presence in my life.

Each firework blooming above shines just like your eyes - pure and radiant.

As I gaze toward the approaching new year, I see both hope and obstacles.

I see hope for our story together.
I see obstacles in what society defines as "wealth" and "status."

I see hope for a bright future for our loved ones.
I feel uncertainty about a future filled with infinite risks.

I'm sorry for dreaming of a love that transcends material wealth. Of hearts bound by love alone in our modest haven.

I'm sorry for fearing that one day I might lose you...

These inner thoughts jostle within me, each vying to be heard. But amidst the brilliant display of fireworks, I realize: though the future may hold countless colors and spread in all directions, it remains a symbol of hope, joy, and especially peace - a profound peace that only love can bring.

I dare not make promises, knowing everything is still too early, and fate is beyond our grasp. Yet, in this moment, as the new year approaches, I want to send you my most sincere wishes. May the new year bring you abundant happiness and joy. And perhaps, someday, when the time is right, we'll have the chance to write our own story.

Each new day will be a blank page. We don't need flowery words or grand promises. Just the simplest of moments: your laughter in the early morning mist, our hands intertwined at sunset, and dreams shared in the quiet of night.

...

The fireworks are still illuminating the night sky, reminding us that despite life's countless challenges, magical moments await ahead. I hope you embrace each of these moments, so every day becomes a new year - full of hope, love, and undiscovered wonders.

Finally... thank you.

For making my years beautiful in ways I never knew possible, simply by being you.



Forever yours,

With all that I am."
