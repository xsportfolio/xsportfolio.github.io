Studio 23

Brand
This passion project involves collaboration with Australian artists to create bold, impactful seasonal artworks. Each piece is designed to represent a specific theme, showcasing the diverse talents of our creative community while exploring contemporary concepts through visual expression.
Jason

People have asked me, "Why did you start this? It seems so unrelated to your usual work." Truth is, that's exactly why I did it. This project is my way of stepping out of my comfort zone. It's about challenging myself, exploring new territories. Sure, it might seem random to others, but for me, it's a beautiful opportunity to grow and learn in unfamiliar ways. It's scary and exciting - and that's precisely the point.

You know, whether this project succeeds or fails in the traditional sense isn't really what matters to me. The true value lies in the incredible journey it's taking me on. I've had the chance to connect with these amazingly talented artists, each with their own unique vision and style. It's been eye-opening to see what art can do - how it can convey complex emotions, challenge perspectives, and spark conversations. Every piece we create tells a story, and being part of that narrative is incredible. The way art can touch people, make them think, feel, and see the world differently - that's the real magic here.

Be bold.

Our slogan, "Be bold," encapsulates both our mission and a life philosophy. It's a call to push boundaries, challenge norms, and dare greatly in our art and beyond. To be bold means creating work that demands attention and provokes thought, but it's also a reminder to step out of our comfort zones in life. It encourages us to voice meaningful opinions, stand firm in our convictions, and strive for our best selves with the boldest effort.
