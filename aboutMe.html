When I'm not pushing my limits under the barbell, I'm out there exploring new horizons - both on well-worn trails and those less traveled. Lately, I've been falling in love with hiking, feeling that soul-stirring mix of challenge and serenity.

Between adventures, you'll find me lost in the rhythm of poetry or swept up in the magic of cinema. And just when things feel comfortable? That's my cue to shake things up and try something completely new, like filmmaking just recently.

Traveling

I believe that life's most enriching experiences emerge from embracing the unfamiliar and stepping out of one's comfort zone.

I constantly seek new adventures that challenge my perspectives and push my boundaries, finding joy in exploring uncharted territories both within myself and across the globe.
Reading

Books are my cherished companions, offering a warm embrace of endless possibilities.

In their pages, I find a cozy nook where my imagination roams free, where I can curl up with fascinating stories, and where wisdom gently unfolds. With each turn of a page, I'm invited to step into new lives, feeling the heartbeats of characters as if they were my own.

Cinema

Poetry

Words paint emotions, metaphors brush strokes, compassion the muse.

This is where I paint my emotions with carefully chosen words, weave metaphors rich with meaning, and fuel every sentence with an ever-burning compassion for the human experience.

My most recent poem:

THE GREEN GRASS
"For even though the sun must set
And love may leave us with regret
The green of grass continues to grow
A symbol of hope, a chance to sow.

So take a breath and let it be
Know that someday, the eye will see
My love, like grass, will grow once more
For you, for us, for evermore…"
